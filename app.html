FizzBuzz

This app takes two numbers and highlights the multiples of the numbers on list of up to 100. It will display fizz for multiples of 3 and buzz for multiples of 5 and fizzbuzz for multiples of both.

Enter two numbers and hit the display numbers button. If you like you can control the amount of numbers with the third box.
